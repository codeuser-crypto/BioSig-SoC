Analog Design

Custom 3-op-amp instrumentation amplifier (gain 500×, CMRR >80 dB), 0.05 Hz high-pass for DC-offset removal, 2nd-order Sallen-Key 150 Hz anti-aliasing filter, right-leg drive for common-mode rejection, and IEC 60601 patient-protection input network.

Firmware & DSP

Register-level STM32F411 ADC + DMA at 500 sps, double-buffered block processing, dual IIR notch (50/60 Hz) plus a 128-tap linear-phase FIR bandpass, Pan-Tompkins R-peak detection, all completing in ~152 us per block measured with the DWT cycle counter.

Wireless & System

Framed binary packets with CRC-8 and sequence numbers stream over BLE/UART at 40 kbps; Python receiver verifies integrity and bridges to this dashboard over WebSockets with <100 ms end-to-end latency.

ECG acquisition meeting medical-grade spec: AHA / IEC 60601 compliant noise floor.

Designed end-to-end mixed-signal ECG acquisition system on STM32F411: 3-op-amp INA (500×, CMRR 82 dB), DMA-driven 12-bit ADC at 500 sps, real-time 128-tap FIR + dual IIR notch DSP in 152 us, BLE streaming, live web dashboard; noise floor 0.18 uVrms - meets AHA ECG standard.
